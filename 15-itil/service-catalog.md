Service Catalog

Purpose:
Provide a list of approved cloud services, configurations, and standards.

Examples of Cloud Catalog Items:
- Approved VM sizes
- Approved storage classes
- Approved load balancers
- Approved logging settings
- Approved IAM roles
- Approved tagging structures

Benefits:
- Reduces cloud sprawl
- Improves governance
- Enables automation
- Supports FinOps standardization
