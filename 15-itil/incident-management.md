Incident Management

Purpose:
Respond to unplanned disruptions and restore service as quickly as possible.

Process Steps:
1. Detect incident
2. Log incident
3. Categorize and prioritize
4. Investigate and diagnose
5. Resolve and recover
6. Close incident
7. Perform post-incident review

Cloud Mapping:
- Aligns with cloud incident response (AWS, Azure)
- Works with SIEM alerts, GuardDuty, Defender for Cloud
- Requires strong logging and detection controls

Examples:
- Cloud resource outage
- Suspicious IAM activity
- Security alert requiring containment
