Cost Automation Playbooks

Unused Resource Automation:
- Detect idle VMs, EC2 instances, disks.
- Stop or delete based on policy.

Storage Lifecycle Automation:
- Move unused objects to cold storage.
- Auto-delete based on lifecycle rules.

Budget Automation:
- Trigger alerts when budgets exceed 80 or 100 percent.
- Auto-stop non-production workloads on budget breach.

Rightsizing Automation:
- Identify underutilized compute.
- Suggest rightsizing recommendations.
