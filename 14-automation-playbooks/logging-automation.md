Logging Automation Playbooks

AWS:
- Auto-enable CloudTrail in all regions.
- Auto-enable GuardDuty in new accounts.
- Auto-enable Config in all accounts.

Azure:
- Auto-enable Activity Logs.
- Auto-enable Defender for Cloud.
- Auto-connect logs to Log Analytics Workspace.

GCP:
- Auto-enforce Cloud Audit Logs retention.
- Auto-create log sinks to SIEM.

Cross-Cloud:
- Forward logs to centralized SIEM platform.
