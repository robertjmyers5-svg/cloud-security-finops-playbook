FinOps-Aware Architecture

Principles:
- Design for cost visibility
- Design for cost allocation
- Design for efficient scale
- Design for elimination of waste

Controls:
- Enforce tagging standards
- Use serverless where appropriate
- Apply autoscaling
- Prefer managed services
- Use lifecycle management on storage

AWS:
- Cost Explorer
- Budgets
- Savings Plans
- Instance Scheduler

Azure:
- Cost Management
- Advisor recommendations
- Reserved Instances
- Budgets and alerts
