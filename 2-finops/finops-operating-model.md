# FinOps Operating Model (Inform → Optimize → Operate)

This is the core lifecycle described by the FinOps Foundation and used in real enterprises.

---

# 1. Inform (Visibility)

Key Deliverables:
- Cost & Usage Reports
- Power BI dashboards
- Tagging coverage
- Forecasting models
- Spend allocation

---

# 2. Optimize (Efficiency)

Key Deliverables:
- Rightsizing recommendations
- Storage lifecycle plans
- Instances/SKU optimization
- Spot/commitment analysis
- Idle resource cleanup

---

# 3. Operate (Governance)

Key Deliverables:
- Policies & guardrails
- Budget accountability
- Regular cost reviews
- Governance playbooks
- Quarterly RI/SP planning

---

# FinOps Personas (Industry Standard)

- Executives  
- Finance  
- Engineering  
- Cloud Operations  
- Product Owners  
- FinOps Practitioners
