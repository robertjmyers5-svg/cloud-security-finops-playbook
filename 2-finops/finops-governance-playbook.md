# FinOps Governance Playbook

This playbook establishes policies, accountability, and guardrails.

---

# Governance Controls

## Tagging Enforcement
- Mandatory tags  
- Auto-remediation  
- Dashboards for coverage  

## Budgets & Thresholds
- Alerts at 50/75/90/100  
- Monthly variance reviews

## Lifecycle Guardrails
- Idle cleanup  
- Orphaned resources  
- Expired commitments  

## Architectural Governance
- Cost Design Review required  
- Storage/networking optimization  
- Performance vs cost evaluation  

---

# Review Cadence
- Daily: Anomaly checks  
- Weekly: Dashboard reviews  
- Monthly: Optimization cycles  
- Quarterly: RI/SP planning
