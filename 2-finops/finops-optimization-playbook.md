# FinOps Optimization Playbook

A proven workflow used in real enterprises.

---

# Optimization Workflow

1. Gather utilization data  
2. Identify optimization candidates  
3. Prioritize by impact  
4. Validate with engineering  
5. Execute changes  
6. Measure results  
7. Report savings
